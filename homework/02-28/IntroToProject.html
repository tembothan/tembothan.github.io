The data set I found is THE PRISON INDUSTRY Mapping Private Sector Players view page with loaded data


About the Dataset

The dataset I found profiles 4,100 private sector buisnesses that use inmate labor. The spreadsheet has fields like:

  • Company Name
  • Primary Sector
  • Annual Revenue
  • Correction Facility Association
  • & more


Potential Visualization

  • One option would be a map of the US that displayed that displayed all the states that provide inmate labor.
    Using mouse over actions and tool tips the viewer of the visualization would see what buisnesses use labor
    out of those states. Additionally the viewer would be able to see the average wage inmates in those states get.

  • Another option would be a visualization of the buisnesses logos that are arange in a bubble chart with their size being
    proportional to the revenue they recive from inmate labor. When the user mouses over or clicks on the logo they would see
    other statistics like the numerical value of the revenue, the states in which the labor is provided,
    the average wage inmates in those states get, and industry/production information, etc.
